Tackling Misinformation

An depth study into the mechanisms of misinformation, resulting in a gameified educational design intervention to teach critical analysis of information sources

Misinformation is a powerful force that can sway entire political elections. For this group project, we conducted an extensive review into misinformation, from academic literature to popular culture, news, historic case studies, first-hand accounts and more. We established ways of understanding these workings via unique mapping and diagrams, qualitative and quantitaive research. This enabled the design of a "sauces" / "sources" cafe and pub game where individuals must navigate untrustworthy food sauces and information sources to avoid the consumption of both horrid, spicy sauces as a penalty for failing to identify untrustworthy information sources.
